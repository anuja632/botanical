How should Dr. Herbi herbal teas be consumed?

Use one dip tea bag in 150–200 ml of hot water. Steep for 2–3 minutes and consume fresh. For best results, drink once or twice daily as recommended.

01

Are Dr. Herbi teas safe for daily use?

Yes, all Dr. Herbi teas are made from 100% natural herbal ingredients and are suitable for regular consumption. If you have medical conditions, consult your healthcare provider.

02

Can these teas help with sugar, heart, or hormonal balance?

Dr. Herbi herbal teas are formulated to support blood sugar balance, heart health, kidney function, hemoglobin levels, and PCOD/PCOS balance naturally.

03

Are there any side effects?

Dr. Herbi teas are free from chemicals, preservatives, and caffeine. They are generally safe with no known side effects when taken as directed.

04

Can I take multiple Dr. Herbi teas together?

Yes, different Dr. Herbi teas can be consumed at different times of the day. Avoid taking more than 2–3 cups daily in total.
